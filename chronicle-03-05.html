Navigation
Symbols, landmarks, and paths are effective instances of wayfinding in urban settings. As such, these features of wayshowing are more effective when spatially navigating a virtual environment. These features provide effective and clear directions, and also allow for the mystery of being lost yet without the fear of complete isolation. Wayshowing provides ways to return.
