Three-dimensional Type Arrangement
The 3D type project, like the strips project, is an extension of the traditional print/2D based exercise. New design thinking must be applied, particularly in dealing with type, a product of print, in a three-dimensional environment. Readability, however, is not a necessity in the 3D type project as the focus is more towards using varied forms to inform composition and visual understanding. Beyond the strips project of formulated right and 45 degree angles, the type project promotes use of letterforms with predominantly curved features.
