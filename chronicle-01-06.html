Chronicle 01 Chronicle 02 Rob Bowman Lewis

Three-dimensional poster
I decided to promote my work/findings through the medium of the three-dimensional poster. The intent of my work has been to discover/suggest how graphic design functions in the third dimension. I defined this by taking the term 3D and using alliteration of three words that start with the letter D. Narrowing down the resulting alliterations to cohesive statements of logical commentary. I settled upon five: Developing Design Devices; Digitising Design Derivatives; Disrupting Design Definition; Demonstrating Design Discourse; Diversifying Design Doctrine.
