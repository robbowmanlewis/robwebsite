Chronicle 04 Preface

Triplicata typeform
Triplicata is constructed through grid design, derived from modernist methods of design construction. Application of the typeface, however, functions with generative design processes. The function of the three-dimensional environment is a key feature of this typeface. Each letter can be viewed from two of the four horizontal faces, in reference to the capabilities of the three-dimensional medium, without compromising of character legibility.
