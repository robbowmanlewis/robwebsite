Chronicle 04 Preface
Virtual reality is a technology which is increasing in popularity within a commercial market. Development of design processes for this new technology will allow it to be more fully realised. Currently, content in virtual reality largely mimics that with which it has evolved from, as seen in game design and user interface design. While features have been re-considered for virtual reality, the approach to design remains largely the same. How then can the design process be re-interpreted with new methods of creation, to explore unknown capabilities of this technology.
