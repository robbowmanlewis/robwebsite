Der Vampyr - Design Communication --

 

Der Vampyr, a historical opera, was modernised by the New Zealand Theatrical Society in the form of a theatre production, held at St Georges Theatre.

 

Themes of this production include drama, sacrifice and conflict. 3D modelling has been used to portray aspects of the narrative and themes of the production to the audience. Such stark, cutting typography and image visually translate messages from the production. A sense of horror can be gleaned from the imagery, which has been applied to both a poster and
banner format.

- Attus Volans
