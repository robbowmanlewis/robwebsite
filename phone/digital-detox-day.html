Digital Detox Day - Design Communication --

 

With rapid advancements in digital technological, the access to a range of digital devices is abundant. More people are using smart-phones, tablets and laptops in a portable manner along with desktop computers, tvâ€™s and gaming consoles at home. We surround ourselves with the means escape from reality. Both physical and mental health can be effected by overexposure to these addictive devices. Digital Detox Day promotes the idea of dis-burdening oneself from our overwhelming digital environment. This allows people to understand the pureness and health of the non-digital environment.

 

This poster series raises awareness through visually manifesting the subconscious battle each individual has with their technological devices. Despite digital addiction being passive, the imagery is aggressive to excite, empower and allow such themes to come to fruition in the viewers mind. It makes reference to cultural, religious and historical themes.
