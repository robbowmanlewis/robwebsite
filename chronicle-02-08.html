Artificial Intelligence illustration
This piece is a commentary on the uncertain future of Artificial Intelligence and how it relates to the process of evolution.Creating this illustration as a 3D model brings the possibility that a 3D printer could physically create the Artificial Intelligence structure, hypothetically allowing it to function as artificial intelligence itself.
