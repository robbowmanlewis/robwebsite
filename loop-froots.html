Loop Froots - Animation --

 

Lupe Froots is an animation project to interpret a serious quote in a humorous way. The quote used was by Thomas Edison; “I have not failed, I’ve just found 1000 ways that wont work.” My interpretation of this was to establish a narrative with a mad scientist having just created his 1000th failed experiment. These failed experiments take the form of grotesque monsters, which the scientist keeps behind theatrical red curtains.
