Biomimicry

Paramaterial
To further realise biomimetic typography for three-dimensional virtual space, Paramaterial, a virtual reality experience has been developed. Paramaterial is an immersive environment that targets the senses and distils the language. This acts as a suggestion towards new ways of experiencing language and new ways of designing. This experience has been developed with Cinema 4D for 3D typographic design, Unity for game development, and the Oculus Rift DK2 for the virtual reality experience.
