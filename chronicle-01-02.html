Gestalt Theory Frame Sequence
This work incorporates two variations of each gestalt theory in a sequencial frame manner running at 10fps. Resulting from this treatment is an undulating visual that shows the extent of variation of compositions that are three-dimensional compared to that which is two-dimensional.
