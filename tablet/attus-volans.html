Attus Volans - Character Design --

 

The story of our character Attus Volans extends from the notion of resurrecting oneself and the constant want and need to become better. It attempts to symbolise the need to understand our existence. This particular graphic series shows the powerful encounter between

man and animal.

 

Created in a three dimensional environment, including photo compositing to create a strong detachment from the world at hand.
