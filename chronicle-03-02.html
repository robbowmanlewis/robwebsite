Chronicle 03 Preface

Three-dimensional fundamentals
Graphic design experiences a continual process of evolution, due to influence from new technologies. Fundamentals of graphic design have been implemented within previous technological advancements to establish effective communication. As virtual reality has similar application as graphic design, translating these two-dimensional design fundamentals to a three-dimensional environment could produce this effective communication of written information while maintaining immersive qualities. Resulting from this combination of methods could be revolutionary experiences, advancing the way we interact with outputs of visual communication.
Three-dimensional fundamentals in the following chapters explore this translation, and subjectively suggests systems of how these fundamentals function. Such fundamentals are based on the contextual review findings, and are refined through exploration and experimentation.
Fundamentals include typographic treatment, grid systems, and navigation. Chapters following discuss design tools, the application of these fundamentals, and further exploration that have informed the frameworks of the fundamentals.
