Chronicle 01
Technologies for visual communication are arising, with potential to be wide-spread in society. Such technologies include augmented reality, virtual reality and 3D interactive websites. All of these technologies work around a three-dimensional framework and as such are far removed from traditional practices of graphic design which consist of flat, print graphics.

Chronicle 02
On the back of the former, Chronicle 01, 3D design by graphic designers for graphic design practices is an evident possibility. Experimentation within this field/medium is vital for understanding application and context.
Each experimentation confronts an issue and demonstrates how design practices can solve problems and the impacts of designing with new technologies.
