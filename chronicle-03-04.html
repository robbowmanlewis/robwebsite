A three-diemsnional space which contains no elements is essentially a void. If one enters this space, there are no referenece points indiciative of depth, location, and presense. In order to spatially comprehend a space, the infinite void requires a structural system. Proportions of the human scale can be used to structure this space, provide immersiveness, and adapt to the viewer.
