Three-dimensional Gestalt theories
Gestalt theories use cognitive behaviours to dictate how the user/reader views and ultimately understands the visual. The importance of Gestalt theory is the cohesion brought to any design through using any of the five principles: proximity, similarity, continuity, closure and figure/ground. Three-dimensional Gestalt theories function differently from print as they explore 3D composition and the function of perspective.
