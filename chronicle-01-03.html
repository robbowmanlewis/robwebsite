Rob Bowman Lewis

Three-dimensional strips arrangement
Translating the strips project to a 3D environment was the amalgamation of the learnings of gestalt theory and the ambition to further discover how graphic design can function in 3D and ultimately in new technologies. There are new technologies acting as a vehicle for this project so its application to medium is justified. The practice of graphic design will not progress unless new ideas are tried or attempted and then executed.
