3D Bling
Jewellery is something that disregards time and circumstance. People find stability and assurance in jewellery, whether it be due to its physical representation of wealth, or its symbolic meaning to the individual. This jewellery is intended for 3D printing, and shows how something purely digital can be transformed into something tangible and symbolic.
