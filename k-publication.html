K - Publication --

 

This publication displays work that portrays three animals; the black bear, the gorilla and the panther. These animals are presented in a variety of ways ranging from the abstract to the ordinary. Furthermore, these designs are represented in different manners such as integrated animals, 3D models, 3D prints. An animation has also been created in conjunction with the publication, which is accessible by a QR code.

 

The book is titled K as in Key in CMYK, representing the nature of the three animals as they all have black coats. Black is a theme that runs through the publication and provides reasoning for the grid design and other design features.
