Biomimicry
Biomimicry often intertwines with generative design, however has a focus more in the reflections of nature, and can use generative design process to achieve this. While biomimicry is chiefly experimented within urban, architectural, and industrial design, it could very well reform the practice of graphic design. Typography implementations could be able to function more intuitively with any environment, providing a natural reading experience, which flat, print graphics are restricted by.
